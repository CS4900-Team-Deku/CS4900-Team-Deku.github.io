Intro

Ocufly integrates object recognition and drone flight software into one package. This allows our product to catch things that human eyes miss. From Search and Rescue officers in dense forests, to police officers in our urban centers, Ocufly is a valuable tool for all kinds of situations.

Our goal is to help law enforcement and other first responders identify key objects in crowded areas. We believe that our technology has the capacity to save lives, and our responsibility to the public is help them any way we can.
